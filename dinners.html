Summer Community Dinners

Food ♦ Fun ♦ Fellowship!

Community dinners are a great opportunity for us to connect with our neighbors in a unique way. Every Wednesday from late June through August residents of the Black Hills, Roosevelt Park, and surrounding SW communities are encourage to get out and meet each other – all while enjoying some free food, games, and music. Check out the details below, you won’t want to miss it!

Location: Hillcrest Community Church, 850 Norwich Ave SW, Grand Rapids, MI 49503

Day/Time: Wednesdays, June 20th–August 22nd, 5-8pm
